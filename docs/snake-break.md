**Controls**

| Key | Action |
|---|---|
| Arrow Keys or WASD | Move snake |
| Space | Shoot last segment |
| R | Restart |

Collect shapes to grow your snake, then shoot them at matching border blocks to clear space. Chain matches for bonus points.
